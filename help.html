What is Join?

Join is a simple application for you and your teammembers to access a Kanban. To make use of a Kanban in the most effective manner we recommend you to do your own research as well. But below we give you an insight into the functions.

How to use it

1.

Go to "Contacts" and add your team members.

2.

Go to "Add Task" and create as many Tasks as you like. Creating a Task is straight forward.

3.

Go to the"Board" here you can find all your created Tasks.

3.1

If you are working on a Task you should drag it to "progress" to let your team members know.

3.2

If your task is complete, drag it to "Feedback" so that another team member or someone selected by your group can review your work.

3.3

If everything is accepted by the reviewer he will drag it to "done", otherwise you have to work on it again and start the progress again.
